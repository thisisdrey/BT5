# Excess reserve amount

## Summary
A bug report was submitted concerning a contract found at https://github.com/CreamFi/compound-protocol/blob/e414160eb8a774140457c885bb099baae528df04/contracts/CCapableErc20.sol#L250-L252. The issue is that the contract increases the values of `internalCash` and `totalReserves`, but `internalCash` is increased by `totalFee` and `totalReserves` is increased by `reservesFee`. This means that the total amount of assets is increased by `reservesFee + totalFee`, while the user only paid `totalFee`. It appears that there is an uncollateralized `reservesFee`. It is possible that the `totalFee` paid by the user should be split between `internalCash` and `totalReserves`. The recommendation is to double check this place.
