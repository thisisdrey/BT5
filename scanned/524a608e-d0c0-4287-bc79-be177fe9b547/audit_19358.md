# [M-11] `Basket:handleFees():` fees are overcharged

## Summary
A bug report has been submitted regarding the fee calculation in the Basket.sol contract. The fee calculation is based on the totalSupply of the basket token, but some amount of the totalSupply represents the fees paid to the publisher/ protocol owner. This means that the fees are "overcharged" as the fee amount is calculated on a part of already "paid" fees, and should only take into account what is "owned" by the users and not the publisher/protocol owner.

The bug report suggested mitigation steps to account for the fee amount in a storage variable, subtract the fee amount from the startSupply, add the fee to the feeAmount after the calculation, and reduce the feeAmount if the publisher/protocol owner burn the basket tokens.

Although the bug report suggested these mitigation steps, it was acknowledged by Frank-Beard (Kuiper) that the amount of overcharged fees from this matter would be very negligible in the long term, and that the proposed fix would not really solve the problem. 0xleastwood (judge) commented that the fees appear to be correctly charged, and that it makes sense to track the fees paid out to the factory owner and publisher and have this amount excluded from the fee calculations. However, due to the added complexity, it was deemed a wontfix by the sponsor.

## Details
_Submitted by GiveMeTestEther_

The fee calculation is based on the totalSupply of the basket token. But some amount of the totalSupply represents the fees paid to the publisher/ protocol owner. Therefore the fees are "overcharged":  because the fee amount is calculated on a part of already "paid" fees, should only take into account what is "owned"
by the users and not the publisher/protocol owner.

### Proof of Concept

L141: the fee percent is multiplied by startSupply (=basket token total supply)<br>
L144 & L145: publisher / protocol owner receive basket tokens as fees payment<br>
[Basket.sol#L141](https://github.com/code-423n4/2021-12-defiprotocol/blob/205d3766044171e325df6a8bf2e79b37856eece1/contracts/contracts/Basket.sol#L141)

### Recommended Mitigation Steps

*   account the fee amount in a storage variable: uint256 feeAmount;
*   subtract feeAmount from startSupply L141: uint256 fee = (startSupply - feeAmount) \* feePct / (BASE - feePct); // note the other bug about only dividing by BASE
*   add the fee to feeAmount after the calculation:  feeAmount += fee;
*   if publisher/protocol owner burn basket token, reduce the feeAmount etc.
