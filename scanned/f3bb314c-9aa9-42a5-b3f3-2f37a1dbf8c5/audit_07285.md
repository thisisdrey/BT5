# Assets may be unexpectedly seized

## Summary
The bug report states that during the process of liquidation, collateral may be taken from the borrower even if they did not use it in the market. This is unexpected for the borrower. The code related to this issue is the `liquidateCalculateSeizeTokensV2` function, `_liquidateBorrowInternal` function, and `beforeLiquidateBorrow` function. The recommendation is to prevent the seizure of assets that were not explicitly listed by the borrower as allowed collateral through the `enterMarket` function.

## Details
##### Description
During liquidation, collateral may be seized even if the borrower has not entered the market with it. Such behavior is likely unexpected for the borrower.
