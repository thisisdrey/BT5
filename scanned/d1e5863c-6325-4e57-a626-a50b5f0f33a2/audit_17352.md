# Potentially wasted funds in `openGlpPosition` due to zero minimum GLP amount

## Summary
The report is about a bug in the TracerGMXVault.sol code. In the openGlpPosition function, the last argument of the Reward RouterV2.mintAndStakeGlp function is used to determine the amount of glp to be minted in exchange for transferred assets. However, setting this argument to zero can result in wasted funds. The recommendation is to check the necessary amount of glp before making the exchange and adjust the minimum amount accordingly. The bug has been resolved.

## Details
**Description**

TracerGMXVault.sol - In body of openGlpPosition(...), last argument of function Reward RouterV2.mintAndStakeGlp called in last line of function's body represents the amount of glp to be minted in exchange for the assets transferred prior to this mint. Assigning this amount to Zero might lead to wasted funds.

**Recommendation**

Priorly retrieve info about how much glp to have in exchange for the asset and make an assessment of the_minGlp amount to put in accordance with that.

**Re-audit comment**

Resolved
