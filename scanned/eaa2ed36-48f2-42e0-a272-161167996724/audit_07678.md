# Misrepresentation Of ETH Balance In NDCs

## Summary
The bug report states that there is an error in the code where the `ethToStake()` method is being used instead of `address.balance()` on line 138 of the `LRTDepositPool` file. This may cause the total amount of Ether held by Node Delegator Contracts (NDCs) to be underreported and could affect the calculation of the `rsETH` price. The recommendation is to replace `ethToStake()` with a method that accurately accounts for the total Ether balance of NDCs, including any rewards. The issue has been fixed by the development team in commit `7db0e43`.

## Details
## Description

The usage of `ethToStake()` instead of the actual balance (`address.balance()`) on line [138] of `LRTDepositPool` may not accurately reflect the total Ether held by Node Delegator Contracts (NDCs) `ethLyingInNDCs`. This approach overlooks any ETH rewards that NDCs might have accumulated, potentially underreporting the total ETH available. Not accounting for the rewards would slightly impact the `rsETH` price since `ethLyingInNDCs` intervenes in the calculation of the price.

## Recommendations

Consider replacing `ethToStake()` with a method or property that accounts for the total Ether balance of NDCs, including both staked Ether and any rewards. This change ensures a more accurate representation of Ether assets within the system.

## Resolution

The development team has fixed the above issue as per commit `7db0e43`.
