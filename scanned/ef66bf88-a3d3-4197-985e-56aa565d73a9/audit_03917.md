# The Maximum Allowed Widget Fee Rate May Be Too High

## Summary
A recent update from the Native team has revealed that they plan to limit the maximum fee rate for widgets to around 2000. This update affects the NativeRouter.sol file and is related to the market makers on Native charging a fee called "widgetFee" from swappers. Currently, the maximum allowed value for this fee is 10000, which means that 100% of the swapper's input token amount can be charged as a fee. This poses a risk of market makers overcharging swappers. The recommendation is to lower the maximum fee rate to a more reasonable range of 1000 to 3000 to mitigate this risk.

## Details
**Update**
Native team has indicated that they plan to set a maximum widget fee rate around `2000`.

**File(s) affected:**`src/NativeRouter.sol`

**Description:** The market makers facilitating trade on Native can charge a fee called the `widgetFee` from the swappers. The current maximum value allowed for this parameter is `10000`, which represents 100% of the swapper's input token amount being charged as fee. This should be lowered to mitigate the risk of a market maker overcharging the swapper from fees.

**Recommendation:** Set a more reasonable maximum for the widget fee rate between `1000` to `3000`.
