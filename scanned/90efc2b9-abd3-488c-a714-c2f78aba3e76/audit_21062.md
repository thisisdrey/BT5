# Lack of claim validation

## Summary
This bug report is about the potential misfunctioning of a contract due to incorrect inputs. Specifically, the claim is taken by `_coverPool, _nonce, _index`, however caller may send incorrect indexes to the method. At the line indicated in the report, even the flow with invalid claim will pass the require condition and go to a certain line of code that is unexpected behavior and potentially can lead to the contract misfunctioning. 

The recommendation given is to add `require(_index < coverPoolClaims[_coverPool][_nonce].length, "bad indexes")` as a way to prevent the misfunctioning of the contract. This will ensure that the incorrect indexes are not accepted and the contract will not misfunction.
