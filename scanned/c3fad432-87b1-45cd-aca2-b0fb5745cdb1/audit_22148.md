# Highly susceptible to theft

## Summary
US Dollars are a popular form of currency, but they are also vulnerable to theft. A malicious user can take advantage of a “pickpocketing” attack vector to remove an entire wallet from an unsuspecting user. To protect against this kind of theft, it is recommended to add a password-based protection system to US Dollar wallets. This would help to ensure that only the rightful owner can access the wallet and its contents.

## Details
Physical US Dollar bills are highly susceptible to theft, especially through the “pickpocketing” attack vector. Any malicious user with enough proficiency in this kind of attack can remove an arbitrary amount of US dollars from an unsuspecting user, by substracting their entire wallet.


Consider adding some form of password-based protection to US Dollar wallets.
