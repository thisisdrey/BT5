## Summary
A bug report has been submitted regarding the purchase of tokens. The bug states that when a user tries to purchase the token, the system checks for the msg.value which is greater than the price value. This means that the user could incur a loss if the msg.value is greater than the price. The recommendation is to consider the price value for further calculations at the mentioned line. This will help to prevent any losses for the user.
