# Possibility of the ownership transfer to an uncontrolled address

## Summary
This bug report is about the transfer of ownership in the Ethereum Staking Fee Distributor Contract. Currently, the transfer of ownership can be done to any account, whether or not it is controlled by the current authority. This means that if the ownership is transferred to an uncontrolled account, it will be lost. To prevent this, the report recommends using a two-step ownership transfer procedure, including the confirmation of the ownership transfer. This will ensure that the ownership is only transferred to controlled accounts, thus preventing it from being lost.

## Details
##### Description
The [transfer of the ownership](https://github.com/p2p-org/eth-staking-fee-distributor-contracts/blob/4bac8f636d7ba14244612bcfb9e85f338feba6e3/contracts/access/Ownable.sol#L47) can be performed to an arbitrary account whether or not it is controlled by the current authority.
After the transfer to an uncontrolled account, the ownership will be lost.
##### Recommendation
We recommend using a two-step ownership transfer procedure, including the confirmation (accept) of the ownership transfer.
