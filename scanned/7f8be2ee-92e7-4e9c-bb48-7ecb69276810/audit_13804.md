# Decimals mismatched

## Summary
This bug report discusses a problem with a contract called VaultLifecycle.sol. The issue is related to a method called "rollover" and involves a line of code that is not producing the expected result. The problem is caused by a mismatch in the decimals of the asset ERC20 and the shares ERC20. This leads to incorrect calculations and results that are too high. The report recommends fixing the issue by adjusting the formula to consider the decimals of both assets and shares. It also suggests updating the documentation to clarify that the "params.decimals" refers to the decimals of shares, not assets. The bug has been resolved and the status is marked as "resolved."
