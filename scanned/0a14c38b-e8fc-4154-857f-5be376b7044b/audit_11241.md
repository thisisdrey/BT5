# LP balance shouldn't be multiplied by virtual_price

## Summary
A bug report has been submitted regarding the `_base_calc_token_amounts` function in the CurveStableSwapNGViews.vy contract. The bug is that the LP balance from the `_base_calc_token_amounts` is being multiplied by a virtual_price, which should not be happening. It is recommended that the multiplication by virtual_price of LP balance be removed.

## Details
##### Description
LP balance from the `_base_calc_token_amounts` shouldn't be multiplied by a virtual_price.
https://github.com/curvefi/stableswap-ng/blob/8c78731ed43c22e6bcdcb5d39b0a7d02f8cb0386/contracts/main/CurveStableSwapNGViews.vy#L116-L118

##### Recommendation
We recommend removing multiplication by virtual_price of LP balance that returned from the `_base_calc_token_amounts`.
