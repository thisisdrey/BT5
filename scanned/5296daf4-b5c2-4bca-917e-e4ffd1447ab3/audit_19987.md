# [M-14] Halting the protocol should be `onlyGovernance` and not` onlyStrategist`

## Summary
This bug report was submitted by 0xRajeev and it discusses a potential vulnerability in the Yaxis protocol. The bug could allow a malicious strategist to halt the entire protocol and force a permanent shutdown. This can happen if the governance tries to set a new strategist and the malicious strategist does not agree with the decision. The bug is related to the `setHalted()` function and the recommended mitigation step is to change the access control from `onlyStrategist` to `onlyGovernance`. The bug was discovered through manual analysis and has been acknowledged by the Yaxis team. A fix has been implemented and the judge has confirmed that the critical functionality has been limited to the highest permission access role.

## Details
_Submitted by 0xRajeev_

#### Impact
A malicious strategist can halt the entire protocol and force a permanent shutdown once they observe that governance is trying to set a new strategist and they do not agree with that decision. They may use the 7 day window to halt the protocol. The access control on `setHalted()` should be `onlyGovernance`.
