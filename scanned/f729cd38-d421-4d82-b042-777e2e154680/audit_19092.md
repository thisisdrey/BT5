# Upgradeable `creditManager` params

## Summary
A bug has been found in the `creditManager` function of the Gearbox protocol contracts. This bug can potentially result in the liquidation of `creditAccount`s or disrupt the functioning of other protocols that are dependent on it. To prevent this issue, it is recommended to restrict any changes to the parameters of the `creditManager` function.

## Details
##### Description
Upgrade of `creditManager` params may lead to `creditAccount`s liquidation or break dependent protocols logic.
https://github.com/Gearbox-protocol/gearbox-contracts/blob/0ac33ba87212ce056ac6b6357ad74161d417158a/contracts/credit/CreditManager.sol#L705
##### Recommendation
We recommend restricting params changing.
