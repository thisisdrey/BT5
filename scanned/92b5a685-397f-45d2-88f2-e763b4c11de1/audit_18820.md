# [M-05] USDM locked unless guardian remove liquidity

## Summary
A vulnerability has been identified in the USDMPegRecovery.sol file of the 2022-02-concur GitHub repository. According to the README.me file, USDM deposits are locked based on the KPI's from carrot.eth. However, there is no mechanism in the withdraw function to remove deposited USDM. This means that USDM deposits are also locked until a guardian removes liquidity. This vulnerability can have a significant impact on users of the 2022-02-concur GitHub repository, as it prevents them from withdrawing their deposits.

## Details
_Submitted by gzeon_

In README.me:

> USDM deposits are locked based on the KPI’s from carrot.eth

However, USDM deposits are also locked until guardian remove liquidity because there are no mechanism to remove deposited USDM in `withdraw`.
