# USDT transfer is not safe

## Summary
This bug report is about a problem in the CommunityRound.sol code, specifically in the reserve method. It is recommended to use SafeERC20 to transfer ERC20 tokens, but this was not done in the code. This can lead to a situation where funds are reserved for an investor, but the actual deposit was not made in the CommunityRound contract. The issue has been resolved.

## Details
**Severity**: Medium

**Status**: Resolved

**Description**

CommunityRound.sol, method: reserve(_request, _message, v, r, s), it is recommended as a best practice to use SafeERC20 to transfer ERC20 tokens. Failed, unchecked ERC20 transfers might lead to reserving funds for an investor that is not actually deposited at the CommunityRound contract.
