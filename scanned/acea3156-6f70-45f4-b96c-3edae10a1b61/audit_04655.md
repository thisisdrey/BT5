# Strict Oracle Tolerance in Spot Trade Can Prevent Traders From Closing Positions

## Summary
This report discusses a bug found in the `PositionManager.sol` and `PositionLibrary.sol` files. The bug is related to closing a spot trade position, which is done using the `PositionManager._closePosition()` and `PositionLibrary.closePosition()` functions. The issue is that the `oracleTolerableLimit` is set to zero between `depositAsset` and `positionAsset`, which can prevent spot traders from closing their positions. The team has addressed this issue in a recent commit, but it is recommended that the necessity of this constraint is evaluated as it could impact trading dynamics and user satisfaction.

## Details
**Update**
The team addressed the issue in commit `ddf6d6a`. The recently introduced variable `needOracleTolerableLimitCheck` assists in bypassing the oracle tolerance check on spot trades when they are closed by the trader.

**File(s) affected:**`PositionManager.sol`, `PositionLibrary.sol`

**Description:** When closing a spot trade position (or more specifically, on `vars.borrowedAmountIsNotZero` in `PositionManager._closePosition()#L682-690`) through `PositionManager._closePosition()` the `oracleTolerableLimit` is set to zero between `depositAsset` and `positionAsset`. This means when closing the position through `PositionLibrary.closePosition()`, the swapped amount returned by the DEX in the `PrimexPricingLibrary.multiSwap()` must be equivalent to or better than the price provided by the price oracle. This check is validated in the `multiSwap()` where the `getOracleAmountsOut()` must be greater or equal to the amount obtained from the DEX because `_needOracleTolerableLimitCheck` is set to `true` in the `PositionLibrary.closePosition()` function.

This strict requirement can prevent spot traders from closing their positions.

**Recommendation:** Evaluate the necessity of implementing a zero `oracleTolerableLimit` for spot trades. This constraint might prevent traders from closing their positions, potentially impacting trading dynamics and user satisfaction.
