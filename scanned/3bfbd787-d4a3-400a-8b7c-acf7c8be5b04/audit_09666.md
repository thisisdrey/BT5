# [M-08] No Slippage Protection for `Decrease/Close` Position May Create a Trade That is Unfavourable to The User

## Summary
The bug report describes a problem with the code that could result in a loss of funds for users. The issue is located in two files, "src/Factory/GmxFactory.sol#L908" and "src/Factory/GmxFactory.sol#L966", and it is related to the lack of slippage protection when decreasing or closing a position. This means that the trade could be executed at a price that is not favorable for the user, resulting in a loss of funds. The team has acknowledged the issue and has fixed it by adding slippage protection to these operations, similar to what is already in place for opening and increasing positions.

## Details
## Severity

Medium Risk

## Description

Opening position and increasing it has slippage protection while decreasing it and closing doesn't, which may execute the trade at a price unfavourable to the user.

## Impact

Funds loss due to unfavourable trade for the user.
