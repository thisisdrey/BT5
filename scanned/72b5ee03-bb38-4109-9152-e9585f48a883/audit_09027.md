# M-8: In the function _handleERC20Received, the fee was incorrectly charged

## Summary
This bug report describes a vulnerability in the function _handleERC20Received of the WooCrossChainRouterV4 contract. The issue was discovered by a group of users and was found to be caused by incorrect fee charging in internal swaps. This means that fees were being charged twice, resulting in incorrect charges for users and no refund of fees in case of a failed internal swap. The vulnerability was identified through manual review and the recommended solution is to only apply fee calculation to external swaps. The issue has since been fixed by the protocol team in a recent commit.

## Summary

## Vulnerability Detail

In the contract, when external swap occurs, a portion of the fee will be charged. However, in function _handleERC20Received, the fee is also charged in internal swap.

## Impact

Internal swaps are incorrectly charged, and fees are not refunded when internal swap fail.
