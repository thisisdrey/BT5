# Contract Vesting does not guarantee existence of tokens to be released on vesting schedule.

## Summary
The report suggests that there is a bug in the vesting contract, which is responsible for distributing tokens at a certain schedule. To fix this bug, the contract needs to transfer tokens to itself. This will activate the vesting schedule and ensure that tokens are distributed correctly.

## Details
Recommendation:
Vesting contract has to transfer tokens to itself to make vesting schedule active.
