# LIQH-1 | Uninitialized Order Store

## Summary
This bug report is about the liquidation handler not having a reference to the orderStore and not assigning one in the params to processLiquidation. This caused liquidations to revert when attempting to access the orderstore on the ExecuteOrderParams params, making it impossible to perform liquidations on any position.

The recommendation was to include the orderStore in the ExecuteOrderParams in the liquidatePosition function. This recommendation was implemented by the GMX Team, resolving the issue.

## Details
**Description**

The liquidation handler has no reference to the `orderStore`, and does not assign one in the params to `processLiquidation`. Therefore, liquidations revert when attempting to access the `orderstore` on the `ExecuteOrderParams` params. Therefore it is impossible to perform liquidations on any position.

**Recommendation**

Include the `orderStore` in the `ExecuteOrderParams` in the `liquidatePosition` function.

**Resolution**

GMX Team: The recommendation was implemented.
