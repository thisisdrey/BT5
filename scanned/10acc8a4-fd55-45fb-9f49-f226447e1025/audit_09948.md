# LP Fee Can Be Avoided

## Summary
The bug report states that the LP fee, which is meant to be taken when users lock tokens using the `lock` function, can easily be avoided by locking a small amount of tokens and then using the `increaseLiquidity` function. This can also be done directly on the UniswapV3 `NonFungiblePositionManager` contract. The report suggests changing the logic for how the fee is collected. However, the UNCX team has acknowledged the issue but stated that it is not within their control as it can also be done through the `NonFungiblePositionManager` contract.

## Details
The LP fee is meant to take a portion of the tokens-to-be-locked when users call the `lock` function. However, this can easily be sidestepped by locking a position with minimal tokens and then later calling [`increaseLiquidity` which does not collect the LP fee](https://github.com/uncx-private-repos/liquidity-locker-univ3-contracts/blob/342c621cc93a13882601b30e547907877f3e3f86/contracts/UNCX_ProofOfReservesV2_UniV3.sol#L318). This fee can also be sidestepped by [calling `increaseLiquidity` directly on the UniswapV3 `NonFungiblePositionManager`](https://github.com/Uniswap/v3-periphery/blob/464a8a49611272f7349c970e0fadb7ec1d3c1086/contracts/NonfungiblePositionManager.sol#L198-L254).


Consider changing the logic for how the LP fee is assessed and collected.


***Update**: Acknowledged, not resolved. The UNCX team stated:*



> *Since this is possible via the `NonFungiblePositionManager` itself and is outside of our contracts, this is ok.*
