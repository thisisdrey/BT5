# Move The safeApprove() Stake to BaseAdapter.sol and Update the Inheritance in Periphery.sol

## Summary
A bug report was filed regarding the inheritance of BaseAdapter.sol in Periphery.sol, CropAdapter.sol, WstETHAdapter.sol, and CAdapter.sol. The bug caused a few issues, such as the safeApprove() of stake in the constructor for CropAdapter not being called for WstETHAdapter.sol, which prevented the Divider from transferring the stake in settleSeries() and backfillScale().

The recommendation to fix the bug was to move the safeApprove() of stake to BaseAdapter.sol, assuming the stake is relevant for all adapters, and to change the inheritance from CropAdapter.sol to BaseAdapter.sol in Periphery.sol. This would fix the mentioned issue. It was suggested to merge CropAdapter into BaseAdapter, but it was decided to keep them separate as not all the adapters need the crop functionality. The linked change was also found to not remove the safeApprove() call from the CropAdapter constructor.

## Details
## Severity: Medium Risk

## Context
- BaseAdapter.sol
- CropAdapter.sol
- WstETHAdapter.sol
- CAdapter.sol
- Periphery.sol

## Situation
The inheritance of the `BaseAdapter.sol` isn’t quite right, which results in a few issues:
- `Periphery.sol` imports `Adapter` from `CropAdapter.sol`, while `WstETHAdapter.sol` inherits directly from `BaseAdapter.sol`.
- The `safeApprove()` of stake in the constructor for `CropAdapter` isn’t called for `WstETHAdapter.sol`.

This means that the Divider cannot transfer the stake, which it tries to do in `settleSeries()` and `backfillScale()`.

## Recommendation
- Move the `safeApprove()` of stake to `BaseAdapter.sol` (assuming the stake is relevant for all adapters).
- In `Periphery.sol`, change the inheritance from `CropAdapter.sol` to `BaseAdapter.sol`.

## Sense
We’ve changed here the `Periphery` to import `BaseAdapter` and not `CropAdapter`. Also, we have removed the `safeApprove()` there, which fixes the mentioned issue.

Do you still think that it’s better to merge `CropAdapter` into `BaseAdapter`? The idea was to keep them separate as not all the adapters need the crop functionality (this is the case of the `CAdapter`, for example).
