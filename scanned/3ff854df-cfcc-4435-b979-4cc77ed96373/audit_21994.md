# AddressWhitelist owner can be added to the whitelist

## Summary
This bug report is about a vulnerability in the AddressWhitelist contract implemented to bypass the daily limits imposed by the Wallet contract. The contract contains a modifier, hasNoOwnerOrZeroAddress, intended to prevent the contract owner or the zero address from being added to the whitelist. However, it is possible to add the owner to the whitelist by first by adding another address controlled by the current owner address to the whitelist and then transferring ownership of the contract to that address.

Exploit scenario: Alice controls two Ethereum addresses, one to send transactions and the other to keep her funds. She uses the first one as the owner of the AddressWhitelist contract and adds the second one to her whitelist. Later, she decides to keep only the second account, so she transfers the ownership of her AddressWhitelist contract to the second account. If Bob, an attacker, gains access to Alice's private key then he will be able to exfiltrate all of Alice’s funds immediately.

The recommendation is to check that the new owner is not on the whitelist when transferring ownership. In the long-term, using Manticore and Echidna can ensure that the AddressWhitelist contract cannot contain the address of the owner.

## Description
The **AddressWhitelist** contract implements a payee whitelist used to bypass the daily limits imposed by the **Wallet** contract. **AddressWhitelist** contains a modifier, `hasNoOwnerOrZeroAddress`, intended to prevent the contract owner or the zero address from being added to the whitelist. However, it is possible to add the owner to the whitelist by first adding another address controlled by the current owner address to the whitelist and then transferring ownership of the contract to that address.

## Exploit Scenario

In order to use a **AddressWhitelist** contract, she uses the first address as owner and adds the second one to her whitelist. If Bob, an attacker, gains access to Alice's private key then he will be able to exfiltrate all of Alice’s funds immediately.

## Recommendation
When transferring ownership, check that the new owner is not on the whitelist. In the long term, use **Manticore** and **Echidna** to ensure the **AddressWhitelist** contract cannot contain the address of the owner.
