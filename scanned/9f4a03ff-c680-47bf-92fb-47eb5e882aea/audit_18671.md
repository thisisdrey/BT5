# [M-08] All AMMs have to be past nextFundingTime to update

## Summary
This bug report is about an issue with the settleFunding calls in the AMM.sol contract. The calls are reverting until all AMMs are ready to be updated. This is demonstrated in the proof of concept where AMM 1 has a nextFundingTime of now, while AMM 2 has a nextFundingTime in 30 minutes. This means that AMM 1 will not be able to be updated until after AMM 2's nextFundingTime elapses. The proposed mitigation for this bug is to not revert at the place mentioned in the links to the affected code, but instead just return so that the other AMMs can still get updated.
