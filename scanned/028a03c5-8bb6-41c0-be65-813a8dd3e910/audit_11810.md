# Facade allows any unauthorized Executor

## Summary
This bug report is about the SwapFacade contract in the BarterLab/argon repository. Currently, the SwapFacade allows users to choose any SwapExecutor address, even if it is not authored by the team. This could potentially allow users to choose out of date executors or executors with bugs, and fees are charged on SwapExecutor.

The recommendation is to have a list of allowed executors and take fees on SwapFacade instead. This would ensure that users are only choosing executors that are up to date and free of bugs. It would also ensure that fees are being taken on the SwapFacade instead of the SwapExecutor.

## Details
##### Description

SwapFacade allows to choose any SwapExecutor address, even not authored by the team. Fees are charged on SwapExecutor. So, it is rational to fork SwapExecutor with zero fees and choose it as an executor for swaps.

- https://github.com/BarterLab/argon/blob/22239aaccdbb78d4aa7ac5c4d0859a9b31c0fc00/contracts/SwapFacade.sol#L87

Some other possible scenarios:
- users can choose out date executors
- users can choose executors with bugs

##### Recommendation
We recommend having a list of allowed executors and taking fees on SwapFacade.
