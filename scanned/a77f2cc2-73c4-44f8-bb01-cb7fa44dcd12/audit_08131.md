# Unauthorized Session Closure

## Summary
The report discusses a bug related to the "reclaim_session" instruction, which allows an administrator or designated authority to close a session by reclaiming the associated rent. This means that an active session, which a user may be using to access their wallet, can be forcibly closed. This can disrupt the user's ability to access their wallet and perform operations. The suggested remediation is to revise the instruction to impose stricter conditions or limitations on session closure. The bug has been resolved in version d58a9af. © 2024 Otter Audits LLC. All Rights Reserved.

## Details
## Instruction: reclaim_session

The `reclaim_session` instruction allows an administrator or designated authority to close a session by reclaiming the associated rent. This means that an active session, which a user might be using to access their wallet, may be forcibly closed. Closing active sessions will disrupt users’ ability to access their wallets and perform operations.

## Remediation

Revise the `reclaim_session` instruction to impose stricter conditions or limitations on session closure.

## Patch

Resolved in d58a9af.
