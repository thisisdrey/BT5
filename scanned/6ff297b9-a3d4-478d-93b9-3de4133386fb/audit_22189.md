# Delayed price increase after target amount raised

## Summary
This bug report is about the FiinuCoin token price. The target amount of 100,000 ETH was meant to trigger an increase in the token price, however due to the ordering of integer divisions, the price did not increase until 200,000 ETH had been raised. The code in Line 207 was the cause of this issue and needed to be replaced with a new expression. This was eventually fixed by the commit 91ff77ca13b74c87ea49014d15016e84b6305bdc. 

The original code in Line 207 read `_wei.div(raisedWei.div(targetRaiseWei))`. This expression was causing the issue as `raisedWei.div(targetRaiseWei)` would be 1 until `raisedWei` was `2*targetRaiseWei`, and the price adjustment would not be made until then. The expression was replaced with `_wei.mul(targetRaiseWei).div(raisedWei)` to achieve the desired price adjustment. This change fixed the issue.

## Details
The [token price](https://github.com/fiinu/smart-contract/blob/7589b9d137bbf902e142e4f0476b06976e390bdb/FiinuCoin.sol#L194%5D) is meant to start increasing after the target amount of 100,000 ETH has been raised. However, due to the ordering of integer divisions the price doesn’t increase until actually 200,000 ETH have been raised. [Line 207](https://github.com/fiinu/smart-contract/blob/7589b9d137bbf902e142e4f0476b06976e390bdb/FiinuCoin.sol#L207) reads `_wei.div(raisedWei.div(targetRaiseWei))`: notice that `raisedWei.div(targetRaiseWei)` will be 1 until `raisedWei` is `2*targetRaiseWei`, and the price adjustment won’t be made until then.


Replace the expression in line 207 for `_wei.mul(targetRaiseWei).div(raisedWei)` to achieve the desired price adjustment.


***Update:** Fixed as suggested in [`91ff77c`](https://github.com/fiinu/smart-contract/commit/91ff77ca13b74c87ea49014d15016e84b6305bdc).*
