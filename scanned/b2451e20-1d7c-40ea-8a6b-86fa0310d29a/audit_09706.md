# Inconsistent Price Precision [samples]

## Summary
The TokenPaymaster contract is having issues with precision. The `PRICE_DENOMINATOR` constant is supposed to have 26 decimals, but the `priceMarkup` is described as having 6 decimals. The same scaling factor is used in the OracleHelper contract, but the `priceUpdateThreshold` is forced to have 6 decimals instead of 26. This causes the threshold to effectively round to zero and triggers an update on every change. The issue has been resolved in a recent pull request.

## Details
The `TokenPaymaster` appears to be in the middle of transitioning between two choices for precision.


* The [`PRICE_DENOMINATOR` constant](https://github.com/eth-infinitism/account-abstraction/blob/9879c931ce92f0bee1bca1d1b1352eeb98b9a120/contracts/samples/TokenPaymaster.sol#L49) defines a scaling factor of 26 decimals. The `priceMarkup` should [have the same precision](https://github.com/eth-infinitism/account-abstraction/blob/9879c931ce92f0bee1bca1d1b1352eeb98b9a120/contracts/samples/TokenPaymaster.sol#L95-L96) but is [described as having 6 decimals](https://github.com/eth-infinitism/account-abstraction/blob/9879c931ce92f0bee1bca1d1b1352eeb98b9a120/contracts/samples/TokenPaymaster.sol#L29).
* The [same scaling factor](https://github.com/eth-infinitism/account-abstraction/blob/9879c931ce92f0bee1bca1d1b1352eeb98b9a120/contracts/samples/utils/OracleHelper.sol#L19) is used in the `OracleHelper` contract. As such, the `priceUpdateThreshold` [should have the same precision](https://github.com/eth-infinitism/account-abstraction/blob/9879c931ce92f0bee1bca1d1b1352eeb98b9a120/contracts/samples/utils/OracleHelper.sol#L106-L107), but it is instead [forced to have 6 decimals](https://github.com/eth-infinitism/account-abstraction/blob/9879c931ce92f0bee1bca1d1b1352eeb98b9a120/contracts/samples/utils/OracleHelper.sol#L74).


In the second case, the threshold effectively rounds to zero and an update will be triggered on every change. Consider using 26 decimals of precision throughout the contract.


***Update:** Resolved in [pull request #428](https://github.com/eth-infinitism/account-abstraction/pull/428).*
