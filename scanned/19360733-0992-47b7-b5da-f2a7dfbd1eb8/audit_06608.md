# Deactivating Reserves Has Improper Checks

## Summary
Bug Summary:

The `_checkNoLiquidity` function in the RizLendingPoolConfigurator contract is used to check if a reserve is unused before deactivating it or setting its liquidation threshold to 0. However, in some cases, the function may return 0 even if there are still borrows on the reserve. This can lead to issues with repayments and deactivation. The suggestion is to change the logic of the function to also check for open borrows and deposited assets before deactivating a reserve. The bug has been acknowledged by the Radiant team but they have decided not to take action as it is unlikely to occur frequently.

## Details
The [`_checkNoLiquidity` function](https://github.com/radiant-capital/riz/blob/65ca7bb55550d30388e2b2277fefe5686ab7e4e8/src/riz-lending/RizLendingPoolConfigurator.sol#L516-L522) checks the [balance of the underlying asset in the `aToken` contract](https://github.com/radiant-capital/riz/blob/65ca7bb55550d30388e2b2277fefe5686ab7e4e8/src/riz-lending/RizLendingPoolConfigurator.sol#L519). This is paired with a [check on the liquidity rate being zero](https://github.com/radiant-capital/riz/blob/65ca7bb55550d30388e2b2277fefe5686ab7e4e8/src/riz-lending/RizLendingPoolConfigurator.sol#L521). The `_checkNoLiquidity` function is used when [setting a reserve's liquidation threshold to 0](https://github.com/radiant-capital/riz/blob/65ca7bb55550d30388e2b2277fefe5686ab7e4e8/src/riz-lending/RizLendingPoolConfigurator.sol#L320) and when [setting a reserve to "inactive"](https://github.com/radiant-capital/riz/blob/65ca7bb55550d30388e2b2277fefe5686ab7e4e8/src/riz-lending/RizLendingPoolConfigurator.sol#L383). The goal of this function appears to be to check whether a reserve is currently unused. However, in the case that all deposited assets are currently borrowed, the [balance check](https://github.com/radiant-capital/riz/blob/65ca7bb55550d30388e2b2277fefe5686ab7e4e8/src/riz-lending/RizLendingPoolConfigurator.sol#L519) can still return 0\. In this case, a reserve may be deactivated or removed as a collateral while it still has borrows. If it is deactivated, those borrows will not be repayable due to the check [of `isActive` within `validateRepay`](https://github.com/radiant-capital/v2-core/blob/43bb77f155c679c35dd30652b6c984c5ad0215f4/contracts/main/lending/libraries/logic/ValidationLogic.sol#L271).


Consider changing the logic of `_checkNoLiquidity` to check that there are no open borrows and that no assets are currently deposited.


***Update:** Acknowledged, not resolved. The Radiant team stated:*



> *We have thoroughly reviewed this issue and acknowledge it. We have decided not to take any action because reserve deactivation will be unlikely in our case since Riz will mostly have two asset\-pairs only.*
