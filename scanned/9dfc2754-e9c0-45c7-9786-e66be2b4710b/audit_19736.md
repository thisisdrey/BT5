# Total supply allocation upon deployment address should not be an EOA

## Summary
The report discusses a problem with the address used for creating JUST tokens. It is not clear if the address is a smart contract, an individual's address, or a multisig wallet. The recommendation is to use a smart contract address controlled by a DAO or a multisig contract instead. This will help ensure proper control and management of the tokens.

## Details
**Description**

The address used for minting tokens of JUST token, we could not verify whether it's a smart
contract address, an EOA, or a multisig wallet.

**Recommendation**:

We recommend you use a smart contract address that is being controlled by a DAO or a
multisig contract.
