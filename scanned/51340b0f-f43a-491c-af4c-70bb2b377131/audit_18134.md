# [M-08] `_decimalMultiplier` doesn't account for tokens with decimals higher than 18

## Summary
This bug report is about the `StrategySwapper` contract in the code-423n4/2022-04-backd repository. It is possible for a user to swap tokens with decimals higher than 18, which will always cause the transaction to revert. This will result in the inability of strategies to harvest rewards. 

The proof of concept provided in the report shows that line 288 will always revert when the token's decimals are higher than 18. To mitigate this issue, the report recommends modifying how `_decimalMultiplier` works and updating the calculation of `_minTokenAmountOut` and `_minWethAmountOut` to account for decimals that are higher or lower than 18.

In `StrategySwapper`, swapping from or to tokens with decimals higher than 18 will always revert. This will cause inabilities for strategies to harvest rewards.

### Proof of Concept

[L288](https://github.com/code-423n4/2022-04-backd/blob/c856714a50437cb33240a5964b63687c9876275b/backd/contracts/strategies/StrategySwapper.sol#L288) will revert when `token_` has higher than 18 decimals.

     return 10**(18 - IERC20Full(token_).decimals());

### Recommended Mitigation Steps

Consider modifying how `_decimalMultiplier` works so it could handle tokens with higher than 18 decimals.

Update the calculation of `_minTokenAmountOut` and `_minWethAmountOut` to account when decimals are higher/lower than `18`.
