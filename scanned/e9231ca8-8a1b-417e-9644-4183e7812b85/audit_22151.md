# High energy usage and environmental impact

## Summary
This bug report discusses the environmental impact of physical tokens and suggests switching to a crypto-based currency to reduce the environmental impact. Physical tokens, such as coins and paper money, require a lot of energy to produce, manage counterfeiting, and transport. Crypto-based currencies, such as Bitcoin, have less of an environmental impact than fiat currencies, which are backed by governments. Therefore, switching to a crypto-based currency can help reduce the environmental impact associated with physical tokens. This report suggests that the switch to a crypto-based currency will help conserve energy and reduce environmental damage.

## Details
The token’s physical implementation requires huge amounts of energy for printing, counterfeit management, as well as transportation. Consider switching to a crypto-based currency to [reduce environmental impact](https://cointelegraph.com/news/bitcoin-has-less-environmental-impact-than-fiat-currencies).
