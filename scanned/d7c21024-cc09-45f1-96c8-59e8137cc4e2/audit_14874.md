# Possibility of manipulation of rewards

## Summary
This bug report is about a problem that could happen when transferring tokens to yourself, which could result in increased rewards. The severity of this issue is considered high and has been acknowledged by the team. However, it is not fully understood yet as the contract involved is not part of the audit. It is recommended to review the business and operational logic to address this issue.

## Details
**Severity**: High

**Status**: Acknowledged

**Description**


There is a possibility that the transferring of tokens to yourself can lead to increases in rewards due to the call to _updateRewards() function. The existence and impact of this issue is unknown, as the YieldTracker contract is out of scope of this audit.

**Recommendation**: 

It is advised to review the business and operational logic for the same.
