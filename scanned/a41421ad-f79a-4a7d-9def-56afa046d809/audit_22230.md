# Incorrect investor count

## Summary
This bug report is about an issue with the Crowdsale.sol smart contract. Calls to `preallocate` in the contract do not increase the `investorCount` variable, which is meant to count investors who transfer funds. The check in line 191 for `investedAmountOf` will also not increase the investor count for regular purchases if the calculated price is zero.

The first update from Hubii stated that the `investorCount` was designed to count investors in the crowdsale, and not early investors assigned through the preallocate function. The second update from Hubii stated that regular purchases are required to send non-zero amounts and the `investorCount` is only increased when `investedAmountOf` is zero to avoid counting the same address twice.

To resolve the issue, consider increasing the `investorCount` variable in the `preallocate` method. Alternatively, consider changing the guard to check for `tokenAmountOf` instead.

## Details
Calls to `preallocate` in [Crowdsale.sol](https://github.com/CoinFabrik/ico/blob/142f43f3bec4afd0ffff3fce06946cf67cb0272f/contracts/Crowdsale.sol#L239) do not increase the [`investorCount`](https://github.com/CoinFabrik/ico/blob/142f43f3bec4afd0ffff3fce06946cf67cb0272f/contracts/Crowdsale.sol#L69) variable. If the intention is to count investors who transfer funds using this function, consider increasing the variable in this method.


**Update:** Hubii replied that the meaning of this variable was by design. Hubii replied *“The `investorCount` was designed to count investors in the crowdsale, and not early investors assigned through the preallocate function. It is designed in this way.”*


Furthermore, the check in [line 191](https://github.com/CoinFabrik/ico/blob/142f43f3bec4afd0ffff3fce06946cf67cb0272f/contracts/Crowdsale.sol#L191) for `investedAmountOf` will not increase the investor count for regular purchases if the [calculated price](https://github.com/CoinFabrik/ico/blob/142f43f3bec4afd0ffff3fce06946cf67cb0272f/contracts/Crowdsale.sol#L186) is zero. Consider changing [the guard](https://github.com/CoinFabrik/ico/blob/142f43f3bec4afd0ffff3fce06946cf67cb0272f/contracts/Crowdsale.sol#L191) to check for `tokenAmountOf` instead.


**Update:** Hubii replied *“Regular purchases are required to send non-zero amounts and the `investorCount` is only increased when `investedAmountOf` is zero to avoid counting the same address twice. Early investors who also purchased tokens through the crowdsale will also be counted correctly by checking `investedAmountOf`, which won’t happen if we check `tokenAmountOf`.”*
