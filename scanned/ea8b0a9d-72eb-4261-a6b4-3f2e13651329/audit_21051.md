# [M02] Users buying Junior Tokens can bypass paying fees

## Summary
A bug was recently discovered in the BarnBridge protocol, which allows users to avoid paying fees. The calculations for fees such as the fee for buying Junior Tokens are rounded down due to Solidity truncation, meaning that in the case of very small purchases, a fee of 0 will be taken by the protocol. This allows users to repeatedly call `buyTokens` for small numbers of tokens in each purchase, effectively bypassing the fee paying system. 

The BarnBridge team was alerted of this bug, however, they did not address the issue. To fix this bug, the team can consider rounding all calculations in the protocol’s favor, which would prevent users from bypassing the fee paying system.
