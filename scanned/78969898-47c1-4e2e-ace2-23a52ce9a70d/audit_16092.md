# M-2: Wrong price calculation in DnGmxJuniorVaultManager.sol

## Summary
This bug report is about an issue found in the code of the DnGmxJuniorVaultManager.sol file. The bug was found by clems4ever and it involves the wrong price calculation on line 647. The code should have the usdcPrice on the denominator and MAX_PRECISION on the numerator. This could lead to a situation where the min amount expected is higher than the swap result under 0% slippage conditions, which could cause a delay in rebalances and potential loss to the protocol. The bug was confirmed by two users, 0xDosa and 0x00052, who agreed that the suggested fix was incorrect and should only adjust for slippage.

## Vulnerability Detail

## Impact
In the case usdcPrice is higher than 1$ (which already happened in reasonable market circumstances). 
Min amount expected will be higher than swap result under 0% slippage conditions. 
The call will revert, which will delay rebalances until usdcPrice comes back to 1$, 
and causing potential loss to the protocol.
