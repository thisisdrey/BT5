# [M-07] `UserManager`: `totalStaked` ≥ totalFrozen should be checked before and after totalFrozen is updated

## Summary
A bug has been identified in the code of a system, where a require statement in `updateTotalFrozen` and `batchUpdateTotalFrozen` to check that `totalStaked` is greater than or equal to `totalFrozen` should be done both before and after `_updateTotalFrozen` is called. This is to ensure that the integrity of the system is not compromised and that the protocol invariants are not broken. This was confirmed by the user 'kingjacob' and was agreed upon by the user 'GalloDaSballo'. This bug is important to fix as it will help to maintain the integrity of the system, ensuring that the protocol invariants remain intact.
