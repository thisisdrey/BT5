# `claimReward`/`withdraw` allowed to execute before staking starts

## Summary
This bug report is about two methods in the StakingPool.sol contract, located at https://github.com/defistarter/contracts/blob/9ffe009ee6047ced669711dde14fe38483abdf7e/StakingPool.sol#L259 and https://github.com/defistarter/contracts/blob/9ffe009ee6047ced669711dde14fe38483abdf7e/StakingPool.sol#L245. These methods could be executed before staking starts, which could lead to inconsistencies in the contract's invariants. To avoid this, it was recommended to explicitly check the contract status before executing the methods. The bug has been fixed at https://github.com/defistarter/contracts/commit/e3f69de3dc861ddc1d8957c83fb89f26651d1ded.

## Details
https://github.com/defistarter/contracts/blob/9ffe009ee6047ced669711dde14fe38483abdf7e/StakingPool.sol#L259
https://github.com/defistarter/contracts/blob/9ffe009ee6047ced669711dde14fe38483abdf7e/StakingPool.sol#L245

These methods formally can be executed before staking starts but after setup.

It’s not critical in that case, but anyway we recommend explicitly checking current contract status to minimize the number of possible invariants.

Status: *Fixed at https://github.com/defistarter/contracts/commit/e3f69de3dc861ddc1d8957c83fb89f26651d1ded*
