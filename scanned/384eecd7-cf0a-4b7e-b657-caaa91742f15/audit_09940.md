# Updating the total supply before minting of new shares.

## Summary
The bug report is about an issue with the oracle Claim Rewards() function in the StWSX.sol file. When collecting rewards, a portion of them is supposed to be transferred to the DAO in the form of new shares. However, there is a problem with the order in which the shares are calculated and minted. They are currently being minted after the variable 'totalPooledWSX' is increased, which is the opposite of how it should be done. This may lead to inaccuracies in the rewards calculation, especially as the number of rewards increases. The recommendation is to change the order of operations to match the deposit flow. The re-audit comment states that this issue has been resolved and the order of operations now aligns with the deposit flow.

## Details
**Description**

StWSX.sol: oracle Claim Rewards().
When collecting rewards, part of them is transferred to the DAO in the form of new shares. However, the shares are calculated and minted after the variable 'totalPooledWSX is increased (Contrary to deposit flow, where shares are calculated and minted first and the totalPooledWSX is increased after). Though the current tests showed that the change of order may increase accuracy up to several weis only, this can be increased as the number of rewards increases.

**Recommendation**

Change the order of minting shares and increasing 'totalPooledWSX , so that it corresponds to the deposit flow.

**Re-audit comment**

Resolved.

Post-audit. The order of operations correspond to the deposit flow now.
