# [M-12] Vault Fees Can Total To More Than `1e18`

## Summary
A bug has been identified in the `Vault.sol` code which can cause unsuspecting users to pay more than they may want to. Currently, there are four different fee types: Deposit, Withdrawal, Management, and Performance. Each fee type is checked individually to make sure that it is less than `1e18`, but the total of all four fees can be more than `1e18`. As an example, if the Deposit and Withdrawal fees are both set to `5e17`, the total of the fees is already `1e18`. Adding in the other two fees could cause the total to be even higher. 

To mitigate this issue, it is recommended that the total of all four fee types is checked to make sure it is less than `1e18`. This bug has been acknowledged by RedVeil (Popcorn).

## Details
Currently in `Vault.sol` there are four different fee types:

1.  Deposit
2.  Withdrawal
3.  Management
4.  Performance

There is a proper check to make sure that individually none of them are `>= 1e18`. However, they can total to more than `1e18` and cause unsuspecting users to pay more than they may want to.

### Proof of Concept

Just taking the deposit and withdrawal fees into account say both of them are set to `5e17`, totaling to `1e18`. If a user were to then deposit and withdraw, that would be 100%. Add in the other two fees and this situation gets even worse.

### Recommended Mitigation Steps

Consider making sure that the total of all four fee types is less than 1e18 instead of individually.

**[RedVeil (Popcorn) acknowledged](https://github.com/code-423n4/2023-01-popcorn-findings/issues/540)**
