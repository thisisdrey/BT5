# [M-05] createBasket re-entrancy

## Summary
This bug report was submitted by pauliax and it is about the function `createBasket` in Factory. It has been identified as a potential vulnerability due to its interaction with various tokens inside the loop which may contain callback hooks. It is recommended to add the `nonReentrant` modifier to the declaration of createBasket to mitigate the risk. The severity of the issue has been assessed as medium by Alex the Entreprenerd (judge), and this was confirmed by frank-beard (Kuiper).

## Details
_Submitted by pauliax_.

#### Impact

function `createBasket` in Factory should also be `nonReentrant` as it interacts with various tokens inside the loop and these tokens may contain callback hooks.

#### Recommended Mitigation Steps

Add `nonReentrant` modifier to the declaration of createBasket.
