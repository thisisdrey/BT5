# PIN MECHANISM BYPASS

## Summary
This report discusses a bug that allows someone to bypass the PIN mechanism and gain access to the seed phrase of a user's wallet. This can lead to the takeover of the user's wallet. The bug was discovered by simply crossing the PIN window, which allows the user to perform actions that require special privileges. The impact of this bug is rated as 5 out of 10 and the likelihood of it occurring is rated as 3 out of 10. A video demonstration of the bug is also provided.

## Details
##### Description

We have identified that, It is possible to bypass PIN mechanism and get the seed phrase of wallet, which can lead to takeover of user wallet. We observed that by just crossing the PIN window can bypass this mechanism and perform privilege actions like getting the seed phrase of user's wallet.
