# [M01] Mint after balance check

## Summary
The `ThreePoolStrategy` contract is part of the Origin Dollar project and its `collectRewardToken` function is responsible for minting any outstanding reward tokens and transferring the reward balance to the vault. However, the bug report states that the newly minted tokens are not sent to the vault, but are only transferred in a subsequent call to `collectRewardToken`. To fix this bug, the report suggests minting the new reward tokens first to ensure they are included in the transfer. This bug has since been fixed with Pull Request #640.

## Details
The [`collectRewardToken` function](https://github.com/OriginProtocol/origin-dollar/blob/bf4ff28d5944ecc277e66294fd2c702fee5cd58b/contracts/contracts/strategies/ThreePoolStrategy.sol#L67) of the `ThreePoolStrategy` contract mints any outstanding reward tokens and transfers its reward balance to the vault. However, the outstanding tokens are minted after the reward balance is retrieved and the event is emitted. This means that the newly minted tokens are not sent to the vault, and will only be transferred in a subsequent call to `collectRewardToken`.


Consider minting the new reward tokens first to ensure they are included in the transfer.


**Update:** *Fixed in [PR#640](https://github.com/OriginProtocol/origin-dollar/pull/640).*
