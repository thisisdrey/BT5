# BRT-1 | Unable to Swap ETH

## Summary
This bug report is about an issue with the trading fee being taken before the amount is calculated. This can cause the transaction to revert and the swap to fail. The resolution proposed by the Bridges Team is to add an adjustment for the fee. This would be done by taking the trading fee after the swap has occurred, or by accounting for the trading fee in the getAmountsIn.

## Details
**Description**

Because the trading fee `tradingFee` is taken before `amounts` is calculated, there may not be enough BNB to deposit into the WBNB contract. Thus, the transaction will revert and the swap will fail.

**Recommendation**

Take the trading fee after the swap has occurred, or account for the trading fee in `getAmountsIn`.

**Resolution**

Bridges Team:

- Added amounts adjustment for the fee.
