# No way of removing Fraudulent Roots

## Summary
This bug report is classified as high risk and is related to the RootManager.sol#L1 contract. The issue is that fraudulent Roots cannot be removed once they are detected by the Watcher, thus allowing them to be propagated to each chain. To resolve this, a new method should be created that is callable only by the Owner of the contract, and which can be called when the contract is in a paused state to remove the offending roots from the queue. Ideas for resolution were discussed in the Pull Conversation linked with this issue and have been verified.

## Details
## Severity: High Risk
