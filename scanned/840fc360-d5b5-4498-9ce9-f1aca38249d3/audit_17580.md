# [M-23] Incorrect withdrawal requested

## Summary
This bug report concerns the _requestWithdrawalFromTokemak function in the Staking.sol contract at line 319. The issue is that instead of sending the amountToRequest for the requestWithdrawal, the contract is asking for _amount, which can cause problems when the balance is less than _amount and only the balance can be withdrawn. The proof of concept shows that if the balance is less than _amount, then amountToRequest becomes balance, but the tokePoolContract.requestWithdrawal is called over _amount instead of amountToRequest. This means that a withdrawal is requested over an extra amount. The recommended mitigation step is to modify Staking.sol line 326 to "if (amountToRequest > 0) tokePoolContract.requestWithdrawal(amountToRequest);". This should solve the issue.

3.  But tokePoolContract.requestWithdrawal is called over \_amount instead of amountToRequest which means withdrawal is requested over an extra amount
