# Lack of checks within the `init` function of `RoyaltyReceiver`

## Summary
This bug report concerns the `RoyaltyReceiver` contract, a contract used to manage royalties. The `init` function within the contract does not have a check to ensure that the lengths of two arrays, `recipients_` and `recipientsBPS_`, are equal. This leaves the contract exposed to a potential denial of service (DoS) attack until the owner of the contract calls `setRecipientsAndBPS` again to rectify the mismatched lengths.

The recommendation is to add a check within the `init` function to verify that `recipients_.length == recipientsBPS_.length`. This will ensure that the contract can not be exploited by a DoS attack.
