# [M-07] Unable to upload guardian shares on social backup

## Summary
The `upload-share` endpoint in the `contracts/src/http/endpoints/reconstruction/upload_share.rs` file is not able to locate social backup ciphertexts because it only searches the `AccountState.backups` field, but social backups are stored separately in `AccountState.rec.social`. This bug prevents guardians from uploading their shares, making social recovery impossible. The bug was found during the integration flow testing in the `SwafeContractTest::testNewRecoveryFlow` function. The impact of this bug is that guardians cannot recover the social recovery key and cannot upload their shares. The recommended mitigation step is to also look for backups in the `AccountState.rec.social` field. The bug has been mitigated in the Swafe project.

We need to provide `account_id`, `backup_id` to identify `$\mathsf{BackupCipherText}$` stored in step 3, and later match it to our guardian share.

However, since `account.recover_id` finds backups only from `AccountState.backups`([trace1](https://github.com/code-423n4/2025-11-swafe/blob/15056ba589b62c52cd4530a78cc5c0be22682e56/lib/src/account/mod.rs# L87-L89), [trace2](https://github.com/code-423n4/2025-11-swafe/blob/15056ba589b62c52cd4530a78cc5c0be22682e56/lib/src/account/mod.rs# L80-L84), [trace3](https://github.com/code-423n4/2025-11-swafe/blob/15056ba589b62c52cd4530a78cc5c0be22682e56/lib/src/account/v0.rs# L246-L248)), `upload-share` endpoint will revert with “Backup not found” error.

**Impact**

* Unable to upload guardian shares generated for social backup
* Guardians cannot recover `$\mathsf{msk\_ss\_social}$` because they do not have corresponding guardian shares in their nodes

### Recommended mitigation steps

Look backup in `AccountState.backups` **and** `AccountState.rec.social`.

**[Swafe mitigated](https://github.com/code-423n4/2026-03-swafe-mitigation?tab=readme-ov-file# mitigation-of-high--medium-severity-issues):**

> `self.backups.iter().collect()` → `self.recover.iter().chain(once(&self.rec.social)).collect()`

**Status:** Mitigation confirmed. Full details in [reports from montecristo, niffylord, and DCENT09](https://code4rena.com/audits/2026-03-swafe-mitigation-review/submissions/F-5).
