# [M-09] All the scxMinted is at risk of being burnt.(Limbo.sol)

## Summary
A bug report was submitted by the user "Hawkeye". The bug involves a calculation of a variable called "adjustedRectangle" which is used to calculate "excessSCX". If the value of "adjustedRectangle" is equal to zero, it will impair the calculation of "excessSCX" and nothing will be deducted from "scxMinted" on line 229. This could put all of the former at risk of being burnt on line 230. Furthermore, it would prevent high value migrations since "scxMinted" would always be greater than the "adjustedRectangle". As a result, no SCX would be available to be sent to the AMM helper nor would there be any LP minted. Since SCX is needed to ensure the proper functioning of the protocol, it would be important to ensure the correct value of "excessScx" is accounted for. The recommended mitigation step for this bug is to insert a require statement on line 222: "require (AdjustedRectangle! =0, “ err”)".
