# Cloned Pets Are Transferrable

## Summary
In this bug report, it is mentioned that there is a problem with transferring cloned pets in the `Pet.sol` file. The report states that according to the documentation, only the Cloner role should be able to transfer cloned pets. However, it has been found that Custodians and Minters can also transfer these pets if they are owned by custodial accounts or by the contract itself. This goes against the intended design of the cloned pets, which should only be held by the same account that holds the original pet. This could lead to a situation where different accounts are holding both the original and cloned pet, and could also surprise recipients of cloned pets if they are moved by the team without their knowledge. The recommendation given in the report is to restrict all transfers of cloned pets to the Cloner role. This will ensure that the intended design of the cloned pets is maintained and prevent any unexpected transfers. The issue has been fixed in the `d3c93f2` update.

## Details
**Update**
**Fixed** in `d3c93f2`.

Recommendation implemented.

**File(s) affected:**`Pet.sol`

**Description:** Documentation suggests that clones can only be transferred by the Cloner role, however, clones can also be transferred by Custodians if they are owned by custodial accounts, or if clones are owned by the contract they can be transferred by Minters. Additionally, accounts holding cloned pets are free to transfer them as they wish.

By design, cloned pets are intended to only be held by the same account that holds the original pet, just on a terminal chain. However, with the ability to transfer these clones, a situation could arise where separate accounts are holding both the original and cloned pet. Also, recipients of cloned pets could be surprised to have their NFT moved by the team at any point.

**Recommendation:** Restrict all transfers of cloned pets to the Cloner role.
