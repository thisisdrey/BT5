# [M-05] `secondaryMinter` may break `plotsAvailablePerSize`

## Summary
This bug report is regarding the RuniverseLand contract, which allows primaryMinter and secondaryMinter to mint NFT. The RuniverseLandMinter, as one of them, will have a limit on the number of NFTs with different PlotSize. However, the other minter is not limited and can mint RuniverseLand with any tokenID, thus breaking the plotsAvailablePerSize limit. This could lead to a risk of broken invariant, and is considered to be of medium severity. The recommended mitigation step is to consider making RuniverseLandMinter the only minter for RuniverseLand.

## Details
RuniverseLand allows primaryMinter and secondaryMinter to mint NFT.
