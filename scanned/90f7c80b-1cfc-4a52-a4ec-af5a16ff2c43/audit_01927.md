# Improper Space Allocation for `PDA` Initialization

## Summary
The client has marked a bug as "Fixed" in the `early-purchase` program. The bug affected the instructions used to initialize PDAs and was caused by not accounting for an 8-byte discriminator in the space calculation. This could lead to runtime errors or data serialization issues. The recommendation is to include the 8-byte discriminator in the space calculation to avoid these issues.

## Details
**Update**
Marked as "Fixed" by the client. Addressed in: `20183672aa44758c6c31e9d858cfac22f5e67d75`

> Added 8-byte discriminator in the space calculation

**File(s) affected:**`programs/early-purchase/src/instructions/initialize_config.rs`, `programs/early-purchase/src/instructions/initialize_sale.rs`, `programs/early-purchase/src/instructions/initialize_guardian.rs`, `programs/early-purchase/src/instructions/purchase_tokens.rs`

**Description:** The instructions that initialize PDAs in the `early-purchase` program use the `space` parameter (`#[account(init, payer = ..., space = ...)]`) to specify the space needed for account creation. The`space`attribute expects the total space required for the account's data, including an 8-byte discriminator. However, the 8-byte discriminator is not accounted for in the size calculation.

If insufficient space is allocated, it could lead to runtime errors during account initialization or data serialization issues later if data attempts to write past the allocated boundary.

**Recommendation:** Consider explicitly including the 8-byte discriminator in the space calculation.
