# [M-04] Owners have absolute control over protocol

## Summary
This bug report is about the LiquidityFarming.sol file located in the contract folder of the code-423n4/2022-03-biconomy repository on GitHub. The vulnerability has a high impact, as the owners of the protocol have full control over the protocol, including executors who perform token transfers, reclaiming/withdrawing any tokens (including reward tokens) held by the farming contract, total upgradeability, instant parameter changes, and one-step owner change. The bug was found through manual review, and the recommended mitigation steps are to make executors decentralized and to add a time lock for parameter changes.

## Details
_Submitted by throttle, also found by cccz, cmichel, danb, defsec, hickuphh3, IllIllI, pedroais, and Ruhum_

[LiquidityFarming.sol#L174-L192](https://github.com/code-423n4/2022-03-biconomy/blob/main/contracts/hyphen/LiquidityFarming.sol#L174-L192)<br>

Owners have full control over the protocol.

### Proof of Concept

Owners have full control over:

*   executors who perform token transfers on behalf of the destination chain
*   reclaiming / withdrawing any tokens (including reward tokens) held by farming contract
*   total upgradeability
*   instant parameters change (no timelock)
*   1 step owner change (gold standard is 2-step owner change)

### Recommended Mitigation Steps

Make executors decentralized.<br>
Add TimeLock for parameter changes.
