# FR-2 | Lack of Access Control For Events

## Summary
This bug report describes an issue with the lack of access control concerning the `notifyFeeReceived` function. This lack of access control can lead to a degradation of the authenticity of the event. 

The recommendation is to implement access control concerning who may call `notifyFeeReceived`. The GMX Team has acknowledged this bug report and is taking steps to resolve the issue. 

In conclusion, this bug report describes an issue with the lack of access control concerning the `notifyFeeReceived` function. This lack of access control can lead to a degradation of the authenticity of the event. The GMX Team has acknowledged this bug report and is taking steps to resolve the issue by implementing access control concerning who may call `notifyFeeReceived`.

## Details
**Description**

Anyone can call `notifyFeeReceived` as there is a lack of access control, which leads to a depreciation of the authenticity of the event. 

**Recommendation**

Implement access control concerning who may call `notifyFeeReceived`.

**Resolution**

GMX Team: Acknowledged.
