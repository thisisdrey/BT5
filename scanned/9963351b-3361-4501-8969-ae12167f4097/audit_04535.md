# Lack of an implementation for taking service fees in ERC20 service requests

## Summary
This bug report is about a missing function in the BandoRouterV1.sol file. The function, requestERC20Service(), is not correctly taking the service fee from the caller. Even though the requestService() function has a check to ensure that the caller is transferring the correct amount, the requestERC20Service() function is only taking the request.tokenAmount and not the service fee. The recommendation is to fix this by calculating the total amount of both the request.tokenAmount and the service fee, and then transferring that total amount from the caller. This bug has been marked as resolved.

## Details
**Severity**: High

**Status**: Resolved

**Location**: BandoRouterV1.sol

**Description**

The requestERC20Service() function is missing to take the service fee by transferring only request.tokenAmount from the caller even if the requestService() function has a check to make sure that msg.value is equal to the sum of request.weiAmount and service.feeAmount.

**Recommendation**: 

Calculate the total amount of the request.tokenAmount and service.feeAmount and transfers the total amount from the caller.
