# Not all amounts of quote asset or base asset may be sent to earning.

## Summary
The bug report is about a smart contract called PutPhyVaultV1.sol which has two functions, sendBaseCollatToVault() and sendQuoteCollatToVault(). The transfer amount in these functions is calculated based on the total supply of the vault. However, if a user has queued part of their LP tokens, this amount is burnt and the calculations based on the total supply are not accurate.

In a specific scenario, if a user deposits 10 tokens, then queues 5 tokens, and the strike is settled, only 5 tokens are sent to the earning address. When the user calls the withdraw function, the vault does not send the quote asset because a condition in the code is not met. The LP tokens that were queued to exit are minted again, and the exit amount is calculated based on the restored balance. The vault then tries to send the vault collateral LP tokens, but it reverts because the PutPhyVault contract does not have enough collateral balance. This is because 5 tokens were sent to the earning address and only 5 tokens were left in the vault balance.

The recommendation is to calculate the transfer amount based on the exit LP tokens as well, to ensure accurate calculations.
