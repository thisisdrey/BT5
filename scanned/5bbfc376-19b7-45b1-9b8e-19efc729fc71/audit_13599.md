# Some distribution parameters cannot be updated after funds sending

## Summary
This bug report is about a potential issue with the updating of `tournamentDistributionAmount`, `otherDistributionAmount` and `collectionIds`. This could lead to some users being unable to claim their funds, as the funds had already been claimed by other users. To fix this, it is recommended to add a check of `require(!distributionEvents[_distributionEventId].amountPaidIn, "Cannot update parameters");` when updating the parameters. This check should ensure that users are not unable to claim their funds due to the same funds being claimed by other users.
