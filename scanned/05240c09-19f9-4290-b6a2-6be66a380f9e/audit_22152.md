# Heavily depreciating asset

## Summary
The USD token, the most widely used currency in the world, has seen an alarming decline in its value over the past 100+ years. In 1860, $1 was equivalent to $28.53 in 2018, meaning someone who had $1000 in 1860 would now only have the equivalent of $35.05. This sharp decrease in value has greatly diminished the usefulness of the USD token as well as the trust of its users. To address this issue, it is suggested that a more stable monetary policy should be adopted.

## Details
The USD token has been alarmingly depreciating in value over the past 100+ years. $1 in 1860 had the same purchasing power as $28.53 in 2018.
