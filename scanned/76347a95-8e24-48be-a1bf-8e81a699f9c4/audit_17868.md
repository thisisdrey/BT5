# [M-02] `VeloGovernor`: `proposalNumerator` and team are updated by team, not governance

## Summary
This bug report is about the VeloGovernor contract, a part of the Velodrome project. The setProposalNumerator and setTeam functions of the VeloGovernor contract can only be called by the team, not allowing governance to call. The issue can be found in the VeloGovernor.sol#L44-L48 in the project's GitHub repository. The recommended mitigation step is to allow governance to call setProposalNumerator and setTeam functions. The bug report has been acknowledged and commented on by the pooltypes (Velodrome) and Alex the Entreprenerd (judge). Alex commented that he disliked how the finding was phrased as the variable 'team' doesn't mean much by itself, but that the finding has validity and a check should be for 'address(this)'.
